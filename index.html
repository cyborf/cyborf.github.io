Research

"Comparing Human and Automated Approaches to Providing Writing Assistance"

Mentor: Professor Molly Q. Feldman, Professor Amanda Zadorian

Writing assistance to college writing is a personalized and scaffolding process, that aims to provide students with the cognitive skills needed in the present assignments and future writing endeavour. As generative language models, like ChatGPT, become increasingly public-facing and accessible to new writers, we are interested in investigating how autogenerative, anthropomorphic writing assistance is received by students.
