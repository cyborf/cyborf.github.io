> ABOUT ME

I'm a Junior at Oberlin College, double-majoring in Computer Science and Politics, with a minor in Writing and Communication. My research interests are: language models, language justice, and human-technology labour dynamics.

I'm currently conducting HCI research at Professor Molly Feldman's Computation for Humans Lab, with a focus on generative models and their impact on student writing. > RESEARCH

"Comparing Human and Automated Approaches to Providing Writing Assistance"

Mentors: Professor Molly Q. Feldman, Professor Amanda Zadorian

Writing assistance to college writing is a personalized and scaffolding process, which aims to provide students with the cognitive skills needed in present assignments and future writing endeavours. As generative language models, like ChatGPT, become increasingly public-facing and accessible to new writers, we are interested in investigating how autogenerative, anthropomorphic writing assistance is received by students.
