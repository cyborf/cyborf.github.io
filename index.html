About me

I'm a Junior at Oberlin College, double-majoring in Computer Science and Politics, with a minor in Writing and Communication. My research interests are: language models, language justice, and human-technology labour dynamics.

I'm currently conducting HCI research at Professor Molly Feldman's Computation for Humans Lab, with a focus on generative models and their impact on student writing. Additionally, I am a Writing Associate at Oberlin's Writing Center, and Research Assistant to Professor Amanda Zadorian's Political Economy research project on Sovereign Wealth Funds. In my free time, I like to hike, game, and/or do arts. I also LOVE cats!
